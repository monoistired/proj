Gungnir is a Tactical RPG video game published by Atlus released on June 12th, 2012 for the PlayStation Portable.

Join the resistance and fight the tyranny of the oppressors!

A story about a land of disparities where the poor must fight for survival a group of rebels stumble upon a young noble girl and a magical spear of great power, with their help they will adventure against the oppressors.
